# End User Guide

Welcome to the **Wise Win Financial Newsletter** — your source for weekly Canadian market intelligence.

---

## 📬 How to Subscribe

1. Visit **[verceltest-umber.vercel.app](https://verceltest-umber.vercel.app)**

2. Make sure **Subscribe** is selected (default)

3. **Check the newsletters** you want to receive:
   - ☑ **Weekly Financial Report** — comprehensive market analysis, every Monday
   - ☑ **Next Week Market Forecast** — data-driven predictions, every Friday
   - You can select one or both

4. **Enter your email address**

5. Click **Subscribe — It's Free**

6. You'll see: *"We've sent a verification link to your@email.com. Please check your inbox!"*

---

## ✅ Verifying Your Email

After subscribing, check your inbox for an email from Wise Win Financial.

1. Open the email with subject: **"✅ Confirm your Wise Win Newsletter subscription"**
2. Click the **Confirm Subscription** button
3. You'll be redirected to the website with a confirmation message
4. You're now subscribed! 🎉

> ⚠️ **The verification link expires in 24 hours.** If it expires, simply subscribe again with the same email.

> 📁 **Check your spam/junk folder** if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

---

## 🔄 Updating Your Newsletter Preferences

To change which newsletters you receive:

1. Visit the website
2. Select **Subscribe**
3. Check/uncheck the newsletters you want
4. Enter your email and click **Subscribe — It's Free**
5. Verify the new link sent to your email

Your preferences will be updated after verification.

---

## 🚫 How to Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time — no questions asked.

**Option 1: Via the website**
1. Visit **[verceltest-umber.vercel.app](https://verceltest-umber.vercel.app)**
2. Click **Unsubscribe** (toggle at the top of the form)
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **Unsubscribe**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email

**Option 2: Via email link**
Every newsletter email contains an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Click it to be immediately removed.

---

## ❓ FAQ

**Do I need to create an account or password?**
No. We only need your email address. No passwords, no accounts.

**What if I didn't receive the verification email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes and check again
- Try subscribing again if the link has expired (24 hours)

**Can I subscribe with multiple email addresses?**
Yes — each email address is a separate subscription.

**How often will I receive emails?**
- Weekly Financial Report: once per week (Mondays)
- Market Forecast: once per week (Fridays)

**Is this free?**
Yes, completely free.

**How do I stop receiving emails?**
Use either unsubscribe method above. You'll be removed immediately.
